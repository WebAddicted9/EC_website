Most Common Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our website's Track Order page or on the shipping carrier's website to monitor your package's progress.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it, with original packaging.

To initiate a return, please visit our Returns Center and follow the instructions. Once your return is received and inspected, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days

Processing time for orders is typically 1-2 business days.

How can I change or cancel my order?

If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team immediately at support@shopsphere.com or call us at +1 (555) 123-4567. We can only modify or cancel orders that have not yet been processed for shipping.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. During checkout, you'll be able to select your country and see the available shipping options and estimated delivery times.

Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes which are the responsibility of the recipient. These fees are not included in the item price or shipping cost.
